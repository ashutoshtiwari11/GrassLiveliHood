About GRASS Livelihood Foundation

GRASS Livelihood Foundation, established on 1st July 2024 as a non-profit Section 8 company under the Companies Act, 2013, is a transformative organization dedicated to uplifting rural communities in Uttarakhand, particularly in the Rudraprayag district.

The foundation is committed to creating a sustainable and inclusive development model that respects and integrates the rich cultural heritage of the region with modern practices. Recognizing the value of traditional knowledge, GRASS Livelihood Foundation endeavors to preserve and propagate the wisdom and practices passed down through generations, ensuring their relevance in contemporary society.
