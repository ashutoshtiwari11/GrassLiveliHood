Our Focus Areas

Livelihood

Rural Development

Infrastructure, education, and resources to empower villages sustainably.

Reviving traditional farming and introducing climate-smart practices.

Women Employment

Skill Development

Disaster Management

Preparing communities for natural calamities with education and support.

Preserving Culture, Empowering Lives

Our mission includes reviving traditional crafts and music of Uttarakhand ‚Äî offering training, employment, and dignity to our youth.

Dholl-Damau

Traditional musical instruments used in folk celebrations. We support artists and preserve this cultural sound.

Maangal Geet

Traditional wedding and festive songs. We document, record, and teach to preserve these oral legacies.

Ringaal Craft

Bamboo crafts like supaa, tokari, kenii, patha, and khamilya. We train youth and help market their products.
